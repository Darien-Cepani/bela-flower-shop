# Bela Flower Shop — Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity
Based on the Instagram profile (`bela_flower_shopp`), Bela is a Tirana flower atelier known for soft, romantic, hand-tied bouquets and bespoke compositions. The visual world is feminine, gentle, and emotional — blush pinks, pastel garden roses, and tender pastel wraps.

* **Brand Personality:** Romantic, soft, feminine, heartfelt, artisanal.
* **Core Vibe:** Blush-toned romance — pastel roses, peonies, and ranunculus, hand-tied with intention.
* **Tagline:** *Flower Atelier · Tirana*

---

## 2. Logo
* **Primary mark:** A five-petal dusty-rose bloom set above the wordmark.
* **Wordmark:** `BELA` in Montserrat SemiBold, widely tracked (`letter-spacing: 0.42em`).
* **Lockup:** Mark on top, wordmark below, tagline `FLOWER ATELIER · TIRANA` beneath a fine rose hairline.
* Asset: `Brand/logo.svg`.

---

## 4. Typography (Google Fonts)
* **Display / Logo:** **Montserrat** — widely tracked, uppercase.
* **Serif Headlines:** **Cormorant Garamond** — romantic, editorial italics for emphasis.
* **Body & UI:** **Inter** — clean and legible.

---

## 5. Website Style & Layout
A digital extension of the shop — airy, romantic, and premium.

* **Hero:** Single full-screen flowers background video with centered wordmark, headline, and two CTAs (Shop Bouquets / Weddings & Events). Fully responsive down to small phones (`100svh`, fluid type).
* **Navigation:** Sticky, transparent over the hero, turning solid white on scroll.
* **Marquee:** Flower-themed scrolling band (Fresh Daily · Hand-Tied · Roses · Peonies · Weddings · Same-Day Delivery · Tirana).
* **Collections Grid:** Asymmetrical category grid — Bouquets, Roses, Arrangements, Weddings.
* **Shop Grid:** Filterable flower catalogue with quick-view modal, search, and favourites.
* **Weddings & Events:** Dedicated consultation banner.
* **Map:** Leaflet map of the Tirana shop with a rose-toned marker.
* **Bilingual:** Full English / Albanian (EN / AL) toggle.

---

## 6. Tech
React + Vite, Tailwind CSS (brand tokens in `tailwind.config.js`), GSAP + ScrollTrigger animations, `lucide-react` icons.
